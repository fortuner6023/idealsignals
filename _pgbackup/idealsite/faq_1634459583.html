IdealSignals is a Software-as-a-Service (Saas) technology provider, so we are prohibited from offering investment services, such as investment management. Securities and investments are offered through the brokerage that the user decides to use. IdealSignals does not have custody of funds, nor does IdealSignals have discretion over the asset, amount, or allocation of any of the user's trades. IdealSignals does not receive any compensation from the brokerage the user decides to use. IdealSignals requires user authorization for each and every action taken through the IdealSignals Interface. The user sets up their own individual interface, selects which individual channels they wish to view, and selects each individual alert they wish to translate into a trade if they choose to according to their pre-defined settings for that channel. 

Subscribed Server is/are which Server(s) or Channel(s) you wish to follow for alerts.
Position Size is how many dollars you wish to dedicate to each trade, so if you enter "500," each position will be equal to or less than $500.
My Contract Limit is the maximum amount of contracts you will allow to be traded per alert. This can be used to prevent exorbitant commissions.
Position Size and My Contract Limit will determine your opening size. Both parameters must be met.
Order Type is what type of entry order would would like, which can be either market or limit. 
Scale Percent is what percent of your opening size you wish to sell at each SCALE (size reduction) level.
Stop Percent is what percent you are willing to hold the contract at a loss for before selling. 10% Stop on $500 position = $50 risk
Stop Percent Type - Choose between a trailing stop and a regular stop market.
Chase setting gives the alert a range that it is still executable within. If Chase is set 5%, and the alert opened at 1.50- then the copy trade button would be disabled if the contract is at or above 1.575 (1.50*1.05=1.575) or at or below 1.425(1.50*.95=1.425).
Restricted Tickers- Select which Tickers you don't want to trade. Format: "TSLA,BA" for example. Option Type- Select to trade only calls, puts, or both
